04 · The Island Vibe
— APRIL 2026
— Shoulder season, decoded

Less people. Same sunset.

April is the turn. December–February is when the island is full; by April the crowds have thinned. Restaurants have tables. Dive boats aren't packed. You can still get the sunset spot at Sairee — without sharing it with two hundred people.
05 · What To Pack
— APRIL 2026
— SIX THINGS, NO MORE

Pack light. Very light.

✓
Reef-safe sunscreen
The corals you're here to see will thank you. Bring it — it's not always easy to find on-island.
✓
Dive card (if you have one)
Saves a morning on paperwork. We can verify most digitally, but the plastic still helps.
✓
One warm layer
For 6am dive-boat departures. It's 26°C and breezy on the water — surprising, even in April.
✓
Linen, linen, linen
Dry-season April is forgiving but hot. Everything cotton and light. Leave the jeans home.
✓
A dry bag
Songkran week especially. Phones, passports, cameras — keep them sealed when you cross town.

Skip: heavy rain gear, a jacket, a hairdryer (we have them), more than one pair of shoes.
